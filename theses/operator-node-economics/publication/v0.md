## V0 Thesis Boundary

Operator Nodes is a predictive thesis about whether AI-enabled production
systems can reduce transaction, coordination, verification, and contracting
costs enough for small, high-leverage operator-led organizations to become a
durable economic form.

V0 does not claim that Operator Nodes already exist as a measurable population.
Direct Operator Node evidence is absent at v0. Baseline public data from SUSB,
BFS, and BDS is context infrastructure only. It must not be interpreted as
proof that Operator Nodes exist, that firm boundaries have shifted, or that the
thesis is likely true.
