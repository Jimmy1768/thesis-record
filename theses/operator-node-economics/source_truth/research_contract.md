# Research Contract

## Purpose

This repository exists to investigate a falsifiable economic hypothesis.

The goal is to determine whether the hypothesis survives contact with evidence,
criticism, history, and reality.

The final output may eventually become an academic paper, book, or research
series. The current objective is evidence gathering, model building, criticism,
and refinement.

## Recognition Discipline

Recognition, awards, and historical importance are possible trailing outcomes,
not research objectives.

The objective is to maximize truth value, falsifiability, explanatory power,
and public usefulness. Do not write for prestige, prizes, or historical
positioning. If recognition follows, it must follow from the work surviving
evidence, criticism, and time.

## Core Rule

Do not defend the theory. Attempt to destroy it.

Evidence may strengthen the hypothesis, weaken it, split it into narrower
claims, or reject it entirely.

## Required Research Behavior

- Mark claims by evidence status.
- Preserve counterarguments.
- Separate definitions from conclusions.
- Separate evidence from interpretation.
- Keep predictions measurable.
- Revise the hypothesis when evidence requires it.
- Prefer primary sources and reputable scholarship when possible.

## Forbidden Research Behavior

- Do not write a manifesto.
- Do not assume the theory is true.
- Do not use spiritual, religious, or philosophical traditions as foundations.
- Do not cite analogies as proof.
- Do not hide failed predictions or strong objections.
- Do not let the working title determine the final theory.
- Do not frame the work as deserving awards, historical status, or special
  recognition.

## Current Output Target

Current target: research base.

Later possible outputs:

- academic paper;
- research series;
- book;
- public essay after scholarly structure exists.

No final conclusion is accepted at this stage.
