# Reviewer Objections

Use this file to prepare for hostile review.

Initial likely objections:

- The theory is just a restatement of transaction cost economics.
- The Operator Node is not distinct from a freelancer or small firm.
- AI reduces task costs, not firm-level coordination costs.
- Corporations will adopt AI and preserve their advantage.
- Evidence is anecdotal or sector-specific.
- Protocol networks do not solve accountability, liability, trust, or capital
  formation.

Do not answer objections until evidence is available.
