## Core Claims

- BLS uses alternative work arrangement categories and discusses electronically
  mediated employment.
- BLS does not maintain a single official "gig economy" definition in the
  inspected material.
- Electronically mediated work involves apps or websites that link workers with
  customers and arrange payment.

## Evidence Used

The corrected BLS article states that the Contingent Worker Supplement measures
contingent workers and four alternative employment arrangements: independent
contractors, on-call workers, temporary help agency workers, and workers
provided by contract firms. It also states that BLS does not have a definition
for "gig workers" or "gig economy" and discusses electronically mediated work
as short jobs or tasks found through websites or mobile apps that connect
workers with customers and arrange payment.
