## Core Claims

- A corporation is treated as a legal person that can sue, be sued, lend,
  borrow, and own or transfer interests through stock.
- Corporations can provide limited liability and continuity beyond individual
  owners.
- An LLC is a flexible non-incorporated organization that can provide limited
  personal liability.
