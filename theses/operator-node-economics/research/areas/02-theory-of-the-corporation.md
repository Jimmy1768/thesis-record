# Research Area 2: Theory Of The Corporation

## Questions

- What assumptions make corporations efficient?
- Which assumptions are technological?
- Which assumptions are permanent?
- Which assumptions disappear under AI?

## Evidence To Collect

- theories of the firm;
- corporate hierarchy and management literature;
- agency theory;
- principal-agent problems;
- economies of scale and scope;
- legal and capital-market advantages.

## Notes

The goal is to understand why corporations survive, not only why they may be
challenged.
