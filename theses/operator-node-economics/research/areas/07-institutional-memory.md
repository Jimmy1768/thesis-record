# Research Area 7: Institutional Memory

## Questions

- How do organizations preserve knowledge?
- Can AI replace parts of management and institutional memory?
- What are the limits?
- What forms of tacit knowledge resist codification?

## Evidence To Collect

- knowledge management literature;
- organizational learning;
- memory loss in firms;
- software and documentation systems;
- AI retrieval and audit limitations.

## Notes

The theory weakens if institutional memory remains mostly social, tacit, or
legally embedded.
